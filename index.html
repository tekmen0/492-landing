Features

Your Motivation

Wanderlust recognizes touristic profile of users based on different tourism types. Tourism types are used to recommend better activities on cities.

Travel Preferences

Wanderlust considers your budget, available time, interest cities with desired departure and arrival choices. All of the hard part of the planning a trip is done by Wanderlust.

Customized Travel Plan

After learning your motivation and preferences, AI-based algorithm crafts a special route for you. Users can see details of the route with simple, useful interface.

Event Recommendation

Wanderlust tries to maximize your enjoyment by recommending in-city activities that are predicted to entertain you. There are plentiful of recommendation so that users will never have no alternative.
